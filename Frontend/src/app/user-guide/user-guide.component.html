Accessing the Login Page


Open Your Browser:

  • Launch your preferred web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari).

Navigate to the Login Page:

  • Enter the URL for the login page in the address bar and press Enter. The URL is usually provided by your organization.
  • or can be accessed via a link on the homepage of the platform.

Logging In


Enter Your Username:

  • Locate the "Username" or "Email" field on the login page.
  • Click on the field and type your registered username or email address.

Enter Your Password:

  • Click on the "Password" field.
  • Type your password. Ensure that you enter it correctly, paying attention to case sensitivity.

Optional: Remember Me:

  • If available and if you are on a personal device, check the "Remember Me" box to stay logged in on this device.
  • This will keep you signed in across sessions until you log out.

Click the Login Button:

  • Once you have entered your credentials, click the "Login" or "Sign In" button to proceed.

Handling Common Issues


Forgot Your Password?:

  • If you cannot remember your password, click the "Forgot Password?" link usually found below the password field.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to reset your password. This often involves receiving an email with a password reset link.

Incorrect Username or Password:

  • Ensure that you have entered your username and password correctly.
  • Check for typos, and make sure Caps Lock is not turned on.
  • If the issue persists, use the "Forgot Password?" link to reset your password.

Account Locked:

  • After multiple failed login attempts, your account may be temporarily locked for security reasons.
  • Contact customer support for assistance in unlocking your account.

Technical Issues:

  • If the login page does not load or you encounter errors, try refreshing the page or clearing your browserâ€™s cache.
  • Ensure that your internet connection is stable. If issues persist, contact technical support.

Security Tips


Keep Your Credentials Confidential:

  • Never share your username or password with anyone.

Log Out After Use:

  • Always log out from the account when you are done, especially on shared or public computers.

Update Your Password Regularly:

  • Change your password periodically to maintain security.
