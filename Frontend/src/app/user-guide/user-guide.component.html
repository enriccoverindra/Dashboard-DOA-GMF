Login Page



Accessing the Login Page


Open Your Browser:

  • Launch your preferred web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari).

Navigate to the Login Page:

  • Enter the URL for the login page in the address bar and press Enter or can be accessed via a link on the homepage of the platform.

Logging In


Enter Your Username:

  • Locate the "Email" field on the login page.
  • Click on the field and type your registered email address.

Enter Your Password:

  • Click on the "Password" field.
  • Type your password. Ensure that you enter it correctly, paying attention to case sensitivity.

Optional Remember Me:

  • If available and if you are on a personal device, check the "Remember Me" box to stay logged in on this device.
  • This will keep you signed in across sessions until you log out.

Click the Login Button:

  • Once you have entered your credentials, click the "Login" button to proceed.

Handling Common Issues


Forgot Your Password?:

  • If you cannot remember your password, click the "Forgot Password?" link found below the password field.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to reset your password. This involves receiving a notification to contact your system administrator.

Incorrect Username or Password:

  • Ensure that you have entered your username and password correctly.
  • Check for typos, and make sure Caps Lock is not turned on.
  • If the issue persists, use the "Forgot Password?" link to reset your password.

Technical Issues:

  • If the login page does not load or you encounter errors, try refreshing the page or clearing your browserâ€™s cache.
  • Ensure that your internet connection is stable. If issues persist, contact technical support.

Security Tips


Keep Your Credentials Confidential:

  • Never share your username or password with anyone.

Log Out After Use:

  • Always log out from the account when you are done, especially on shared or public computers.

Update Your Password Regularly:

  • Change your password periodically to maintain security.

Account Info



Name

This field displays your full name as it is registered within the system. It is essential to ensure that this information is accurate and up-to-date as it is used for identification and communication purposes.


Email

This field shows the email address associated with your account. The email address is a vital piece of information as it is used for logging in, receiving notifications, and password recovery. Ensuring that this email is current and accessible is important for maintaining account security and receiving timely updates.


Unit

This field indicates the organizational unit or department you belong to. This information helps in categorizing users based on their functional areas within the organization. It aids in ensuring that communication and permissions are appropriately managed according to departmental roles and responsibilities.


Role

This field shows your role within the organization, such as Admin, DO, AO or IM. The role assigned to your account determines the level of access and permissions you have on the platform. It is crucial for maintaining security and proper access controls, ensuring that each user has the appropriate level of access required for their duties.

Search Non-Conformance Form



Introduction

This guide provides comprehensive instructions for users to effectively search for Non-Conformance Reports (NCRs) using the search functionality provided in the system. Ensure that you have the necessary permissions to access and search the NCR database.

Accessing the Search NCR Page

  1. Open your preferred web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari).
  2. Enter the URL for the NCR search page provided by your organization.
  3. The page title "Search NON-CONFORMANCE REPORT" should be visible.

Using the Search Functionality

Search Parameters:

You can search for NCRs using either the TO UIC or NCR INIT ID fields. Enter the appropriate search term in the search input field.


Initiate the Search:

After entering the search term, click the Search button or press enter. The system will process your request and display the relevant NCRs in a table format.

    Understanding the Search Results

    The search results are displayed in a table with the following columns:

    Column Description
    NCR Init ID Unique identifier for the NCR initiation.
    Regulation Based Basis of the regulation related to the NCR.
    Subject Subject or title of the NCR.
    Audit Plan No Number assigned to the audit plan.
    NCR No The specific NCR number.
    Issued Date Date when the NCR was issued.
    Responsibility Office Office responsible for addressing the NCR.
    Audit Type Type of audit performed.
    To UIC Unique identifier code for the unit.
    Require Condition Reference Reference for the required condition.
    Level Finding Level of the finding (e.g., 1, 2, 3).
    Problem Analysis Analysis of the problem identified.
    Answer Due Date Date by which a response is due.
    Issued IAN Identifier for issued IAN.
    IAN No Number for the issued IAN.
    Encountered Condition Condition encountered during the audit.
    Audit By Name of the auditor.
    Audit Date Date of the audit.
    Acknowledge By Person who acknowledged the NCR.
    Acknowledge Date Date of acknowledgment.
    Status Current status of the NCR.
    Temporary Link Link for temporary access.
    Action Actions available (e.g., Preview, Edit).

Performing Actions on Search Results

Preview an NCR:

Click the Preview button next to the NCR you wish to view. This will open a detailed view of the selected NCR.

Edit an NCR:

Click the Edit button next to the NCR you wish to modify. This will redirect you to the edit page where you can update the NCR details.

Export to Excel:

Click the Export to Excel button to download the search results in an Excel format for offline analysis and record-keeping.

Submitting the Form

  1. Review: Thoroughly review all entered information for accuracy and completeness. Ensure all required fields are filled out.
